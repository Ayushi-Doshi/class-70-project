i had visited on Thursday,12th September 19.

Congratulations to our newly elected student council members. The investiture ceremony for the same was conducted on Thursday,12th September 19. It started with invoking blessings from Maa Saraswati, welcoming guests, parents, teachers and students. Chief guest Ms. Jagruti Gala inspired the students with her encouraging words and awarded the newly elected members with badges and sashes. The old student council shared their experiences and motivated the present student leaders. Oath taking ceremony was led by our principal Mr. Vipin Baria followed by a vote of thanks.



Amrit Alumni Meet 2019-20

i havnt visited it but it was held in my school

On 28th of December, 2019, Amrit Parivaar hosted a very special event and announced the launch of it’s first ever, Amrit Alumni Association! More than 70 alumni joined the significant day. They were received with a booming, musical welcome at the new campus to form the Amrit Alumni Association under the vision and leadership of Mr Kavish Gadia, who is set to usher the school into a new future. This new future includes a dream of collaboration with all aspects of community and industry, as we can only achieve a grand school when all stakeholders are actively engaged. In this same spirit, Amrit expects that it’s Alumni are the best poised to support this expansion and growth. Who better to talk about the work than the ones who have experienced it for themselves. We now wait to see the magic spread through the creative work of the Amrit Alumni Association to lead the vision of Amrit vidyalaya!

School Acheivements

National level winners in Katha workshop

Amrit Vidyalaya is proud to share that two of our students Mahek Mahajan and Vishal Gupta won at the National challenge round at Katha Utsav, a search for excellence in creative writing workshops. The workshop was scheduled from 26th to 29th December 2019 at Shiv Nadar School Gurgaon. Five of Amrit students- Drashti Bagdah, Khushi Gandhi, Mahek Mahajan, Fahad Gora and Vishal Gupta were selected to attend the workshop. Students from Bangalore, Hyderabad, Nagaland, Tamilnadu, Nagpur, Jamnagar, all across the country were part of this workshop. After 3 days of mentoring and tutoring, on the 4th day the students were given a topic to write a story/poem on it. The work had to be original and written on that spot in the allotted time. As usual Amrit students amazed everyone with their work.

news about my school

  • clay modeling competition goin to be held

    Clay modelling is a part of younger children’s holistic learning at Amrit Vidyalaya. It develops a child’s motor skills and helps him/her to learn creative problem-solving. To keep their interest alive, competition on the same was held on the 30th August 19 on the theme of ”My friend Ganesha” for kindergarten 1 and 2. Children made many models related to Lord Ganesha’s idol, his ladoo, Mushakraj (mouse) and decoration related to Ganesh Sthapna etc. They were super excited to show their creativity through clay.

  • rang - tarang art event

    Amrit Vidyalaya is a hallmark for the last 24 years to bring the best of opportunities in academics and cultural development in children of this region. An inter-school Art event, RANGTARANG, was hosted in Amrit campus on the 14th of September, 2019 for grades 1 to 9 in different categories with different themes. The Art Competition was a successful one which gave children a chance to express their talents, to bring glory to their school and to form synergy and positive relationships with other schools children. A well-known retired Art teacher of the region Mr. Darji of PKS high school was one of the judges and other two judges were appointed from other schools through a lottery system . Each and everyone was so happy with Amrit hospitality and the way of selection process of winners.
